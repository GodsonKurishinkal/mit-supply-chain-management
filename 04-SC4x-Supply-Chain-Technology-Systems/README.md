# SC4x: Supply Chain Technology and Systems

## Course Overview
This course covers the technology landscape in supply chains, including ERP systems, information flow management, and digital transformation initiatives.

## Topics Covered

### Week 1-3: Supply Chain Information Systems
- ERP systems overview
- Supply chain execution systems
- Data management

### Week 4-6: Information Flow
- Information sharing mechanisms
- Real-time visibility
- Data integration

### Week 7-9: Emerging Technologies
- IoT in supply chains
- Blockchain applications
- AI and machine learning

### Week 10-13: Digital Transformation
- Industry 4.0
- Digital supply chain strategies
- Implementation challenges

## Learning Objectives
- [ ] Understand supply chain technology landscape
- [ ] Evaluate technology solutions
- [ ] Design information flow systems
- [ ] Plan digital transformation initiatives

## Key Technologies
- Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
- Warehouse Management Systems (WMS)
- Transportation Management Systems (TMS)
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Blockchain
- Artificial Intelligence
