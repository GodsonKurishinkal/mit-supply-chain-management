# SC3x: Supply Chain Dynamics

## Course Overview
This course explores the dynamic behavior of supply chains, including the bullwhip effect, supply chain coordination mechanisms, and risk management strategies.

## Topics Covered

### Week 1-3: Supply Chain Dynamics
- System dynamics fundamentals
- Feedback loops in supply chains
- Dynamic behavior analysis

### Week 4-6: Bullwhip Effect
- Causes of demand amplification
- Quantifying the bullwhip effect
- Mitigation strategies

### Week 7-9: Supply Chain Coordination
- Information sharing
- Contract mechanisms
- Incentive alignment

### Week 10-13: Risk Management
- Supply chain disruptions
- Risk identification and assessment
- Resilience strategies

## Learning Objectives
- [ ] Understand supply chain dynamics and feedback loops
- [ ] Analyze and mitigate the bullwhip effect
- [ ] Design coordination mechanisms
- [ ] Develop risk management strategies

## Key Concepts
- Beer Game simulation
- Vendor-managed inventory (VMI)
- Collaborative planning (CPFR)
- Supply chain resilience
