# CFx: Comprehensive Final Exam

## Overview
The Comprehensive Final Exam (CFx) is a proctored examination that tests your knowledge across all five courses in the MicroMasters program.

## Exam Structure
- **Duration:** Proctored examination
- **Coverage:** All SC1x through SC5x material
- **Format:** Multiple choice, calculations, and case analysis

## Topics to Review

### SC1x: Supply Chain Fundamentals
- Demand forecasting methods
- Inventory management (EOQ, safety stock)
- ABC analysis and segmentation

### SC2x: Supply Chain Design
- Facility location models
- Network optimization
- Capacity planning

### SC3x: Supply Chain Dynamics
- Bullwhip effect
- Coordination mechanisms
- Risk management

### SC4x: Supply Chain Technology
- ERP systems
- Digital transformation
- Emerging technologies

### SC5x: Supply Chain Analytics
- Predictive analytics
- Optimization algorithms
- Machine learning applications
