# SC1x: Supply Chain Fundamentals

## Course Overview
This course provides foundational knowledge in supply chain management, covering demand forecasting, inventory management, and supply chain network planning.

## Topics Covered

### Week 1-3: Demand Forecasting
- Time series decomposition
- Moving averages and exponential smoothing
- Forecast accuracy metrics (MAD, MAPE, MSE)

### Week 4-6: Inventory Management
- Economic Order Quantity (EOQ)
- Newsvendor model
- Safety stock and service levels

### Week 7-9: Supply Chain Segmentation
- ABC analysis
- Customer segmentation
- Product classification

### Week 10-13: Network Planning Basics
- Supply chain network structure
- Inventory positioning
- Multi-echelon systems

## Learning Objectives
- [ ] Understand demand patterns and forecasting methods
- [ ] Apply inventory optimization techniques
- [ ] Analyze supply chain trade-offs
- [ ] Use data-driven approaches for decision making

## Key Formulas
- EOQ = √(2DS/H)
- Safety Stock = z × σ × √L
- Forecast Error = Actual - Forecast
