# SC2x: Supply Chain Design

## Course Overview
This course focuses on the strategic design of supply chain networks, including facility location decisions, capacity planning, and network optimization.

## Topics Covered

### Week 1-3: Network Design Fundamentals
- Supply chain network structure
- Design trade-offs
- Total cost modeling

### Week 4-6: Facility Location
- Center of gravity method
- Mixed-integer programming
- Multi-facility location

### Week 7-9: Capacity Planning
- Capacity strategies (lead, lag, match)
- Economies of scale
- Flexibility vs. efficiency

### Week 10-13: Network Optimization
- Transportation models
- Transshipment problems
- Global network design

## Learning Objectives
- [ ] Design optimal supply chain networks
- [ ] Apply facility location models
- [ ] Evaluate capacity planning strategies
- [ ] Use optimization tools for network design

## Key Concepts
- Fixed vs. variable costs
- Service level constraints
- Risk pooling through network design
- Make vs. buy decisions
