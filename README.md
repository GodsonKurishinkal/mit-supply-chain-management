## ğŸ“ Course Details

### SC1x: Supply Chain Fundamentals
- **Focus:** Demand forecasting, inventory management, network planning
- **Skills:** Statistical forecasting, safety stock optimization, supply chain network design

### SC2x: Supply Chain Design
- **Focus:** Network design, facility location, capacity planning
- **Skills:** Optimization models, scenario analysis, strategic planning

### SC3x: Supply Chain Dynamics
- **Focus:** Bullwhip effect, supply chain coordination, risk management
- **Skills:** System dynamics, simulation, risk mitigation strategies

### SC4x: Supply Chain Technology and Systems
- **Focus:** ERP systems, information flow, digital transformation
- **Skills:** Technology evaluation, system integration, digital supply chains

### SC5x: Supply Chain Analytics
- **Focus:** Advanced analytics, machine learning in supply chains
- **Skills:** Predictive analytics, optimization algorithms, data-driven decision making

### CFx: Comprehensive Final Exam
- **Focus:** Integration of all course concepts
- **Format:** Proctored examination covering all program material
